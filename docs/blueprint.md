# **App Name**: Life Compass

## Core Features:

- Interactive Input: Interactive sliders to rate satisfaction in Career, Finances, Health, Family, Relationships, Personal Growth, Fun, and Environment.
- Dynamic Polygon: A dynamic polygon visualization representing the Wheel of Life, updating in real-time with slider adjustments, offering immediate visual feedback.
- General Score Calculation: Dynamically calculate and display a general well-being score based on the average of all slider values.
- AI-Powered Recommendations: Use a tool that offers personalized recommendations based on areas with low scores, providing actionable advice for improvement.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: HSL(210, 60%, 50%) converted to RGB hex: #4D94FF for a sense of calm and focus.
- Background color: HSL(210, 20%, 95%) converted to RGB hex: #F0F5FF, to give off a modern design and not be a distraction for the visualization.
- Accent color: HSL(180, 50%, 50%) converted to RGB hex: #40BFBF to guide focus and bring calm balance into the page
- Font pairing: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for both headlines and body text due to its modern and readable design, enhancing the app's clarity.
- Simple, outline-style icons for each category, visually connecting sliders to corresponding aspects of life.
- Clean, single-page layout with the polygon visualization as the central element, flanked by sliders and recommendations below.
- Smooth transitions for polygon updates and score changes, providing a seamless user experience.
